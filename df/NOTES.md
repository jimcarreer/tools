Hack Notes

# Custom Script
[DFHack]# reveal-des

Reveals designated tiles, useful
for ignoring damp / warm stone
cancellations

# Paint rough wall:
[DFHack]# tiletypes
tiletypes> paint sp normal
tiletypes> paint sh wall
tiletypes> paint m stone granite
tiletypes> run
tiletypes> q

Material must be all lower case.
